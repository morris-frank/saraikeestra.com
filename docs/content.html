About Me

Born and raised in Amsterdam, I'm passionate about understanding human health through an evolutionary lens. My research explores how our first 1000 days shape our health, with a particular focus on thyroid function and its evolutionary significance.

Notable Work

My research has contributed to understanding global COVID-19 vaccine inequity, clinical trial transparency, and evolutionary perspectives on human health. I've published in leading journals including Nature Human Behaviour, BMJ Global Health, and The Lancet Planetary Health.
